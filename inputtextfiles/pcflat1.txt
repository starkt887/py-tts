Namaskar doston, Bollywood ki desi girl Priyanka Chopra ko toh aap jaante hi honge. Haal hi mein unhone Mumbai mein apne aalishan flats bech diye hain, jisse woh karodon ki deal karke malamal ho gayi hain. Toh chaliye jaante hain poori khabar.
Priyanka Chopra ne Mumbai ke Lokhandwala Complex ke Oberoi Sky Gardens mein apne chaar luxury flats beche hain. In flats ki kul keemat 16.17 crore rupaye se bhi zyada hai. Inme se teen flats 18vi manzil par hain, jabki ek flat 19vi manzil par hai.
Flat number 1801/A, jise Shruti Gaurav Sachdeva ne 3 crore 45 lakh 11 hazar 500 rupaye mein kharida.
Flat number 1801/C, jise Sneha Dang Sachdeva ne 2 crore 85 lakh rupaye mein kharida.
Flat number 1901/C, jise Raunak Triloka Sachdeva ko 3 crore 52 lakh rupaye mein becha gaya.
Aur ek jodi flat (1801/B aur 1901/B), jise Rajni Triloka Sachdeva ne 6 crore 35 lakh rupaye mein kharida.
In flats ke liye kharidaron ne lakhon rupaye ka stamp shulk bhi chukaya hai. Priyanka Chopra ke in flats ki bikri ne Mumbai ke real estate bazaar mein halchal macha di hai.
Priyanka Chopra ke is kadam se kai sawal uth rahe hain. Kya unhone Mumbai se poori tarah nata tod liya hai? Ya yeh sirf ek nivesh ka mamla hai? Filhaal, Priyanka Chopra ya unke pravakta ki aur se koi adhikarik bayan nahi aaya hai.
